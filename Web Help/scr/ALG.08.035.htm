Transport Bar

The transport bar contains the audio playback controls. To show or hide the bar, use "View -> More Bars ->Transport Bar". Due to constant development in design and features, the bars may appear different in the actual program.

During Playback, the Play/Pause button changes to:  

  • Play/Pause toggles between play and pause.
  • Stop halts playback and returns the audio cursor to the beginning of the audio.
  • Rewind jumps to the beginning of the audio, or to the start of the loop, without halting playback.
  • Loop toggles looping of the selection if one exists, or of the entire audio otherwise.
  • Record Option is used to record from mic.
