Volume Bar

 

The volume bar contains a slider that controls Signal Studio's playback volume. This slider only affects audio output from the current instance of Signal Studio, and is distinct from the Windows volume control, or the equivalent control provided by your audio driver.

The volume bar also includes a mute button , which lets you temporarily silence Signal Studio without losing your volume setting.

When mute is enabled, sliding the volume bar will have no effect untill the mute is released.

To show or hide the bar, use " View -> More Bars -> Volume Bar "
